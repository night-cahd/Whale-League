About WHALE League

This is a semi-competitive league that will be run tournament style. The main goal of this league is play fun and competitive valorant with your friends. Apply for a spot as a captain to fill your team of 5 with whoever you want!

League Rules:

1. If you are not rostered on a team you are not allowed to participate.
2. Each team is assigned 5 main players and 2 stand-ins, a third stand in spot will be provided at the conclusion of pool play.
3. Any form of cheating is strictly prohibited, if any evidence is found suggesting of it that players team will forfeit their season.
4. Servers will only be hosted in NA unless both teams are outside of NA.
5. You have 15 minutes after the scheduled time to arrive, if not the game is forfeited by which ever team has fewer players. (you are allowed to 4v5).
